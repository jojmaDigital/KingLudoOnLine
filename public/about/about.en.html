❓ What Is Ludo King?

Ludo King is the online version of the traditional board game. You’ll play on a square board with four colored areas—red, blue, green, and yellow. Each player starts with four tokens, and the aim is to get them all to the center “home.”

The best part? It’s free to play, requires no downloads, and works perfectly on your computer, tablet, or phone. It’s fun, easy to learn, and perfect for kids, families, and friends.

🎲 How to Play Ludo King

Your mission: move all four tokens from your starting base to the home area before your opponents.

1. Set Up the Board

  • Each player chooses a color: red, blue, green, or yellow.
  • All four tokens start in the matching colored base.
  • The home area is the star-shaped center of the board.

2. Roll the Dice

  • Click or tap the dice to roll.
  • You need a six to bring a token out of the base.
  • Each roll moves a token forward the number shown.

3. Move Your Tokens

Tokens travel around the board clockwise. If you land on an opponent’s token, you send it back to their base.

4. Reach Home

Guide each of your four tokens safely to the home path. The first to bring all four wins! 🏆

🎮 Controls

Mouse or Touch: Tap or click the dice to roll, then select a token to move.

Keyboard: Use arrow keys to highlight tokens and press Enter to move. Space bar rolls the dice quickly.

Quick Restart: Press "R" to start a new match instantly.

💡 Tips and Tricks for Ludo King

1. Protect Your Tokens

Keep tokens grouped together to reduce the risk of being captured.

2. Prioritize Sixes

Bring tokens out of your base quickly whenever you roll a six.

3. Block Opponents

Use your tokens to stop opponents on key spaces and slow them down.

4. Spread Out

Don’t keep all your tokens in one place—control different areas of the board.

5. Think Ahead

Plan your moves carefully to avoid getting sent back to base.
